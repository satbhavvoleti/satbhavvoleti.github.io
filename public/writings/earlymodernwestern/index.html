Notes on Western Modern Philosophy

· 01 Aug 2020 ·

While reading various early modern texts of Europe, I made some (highly unoriginal) notes by an almost collage method; pulling from many secondaries on major figures of the time period. The notes are highly incomplete and are a slow work in progress, but the main sources were Jeffrey Tlumak’s Classical Modern Philosophy, Dan Garber’s work on Decartes, and Michael Ayer’s Locke: Epistemology and Ontology. Maybe they can be of some help for skimming through before an exam?
